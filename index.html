K-12 Funding in the U.S.

Each state in the U.S. differs in the amount per student it spends on public school education.

There is a stark difference between the amount of funding each state allocates per student in each state. Depending on the district, a student in Utah could be receiving $15,000 less than a student in New York, amounting to about 1/3 the amount of resources.

Four states spend less than $8,000, with Utah spending the least at $7,006.

Five states spend over than $19,000, with New York spending the most at $21,818.
